Governance

Findings that remain reviewable and defensible

Neraium is intended to support disciplined operational review. The goal is not uncontrolled automation, but earlier insight that can be evaluated by reliability and engineering teams.

Governance Model

Designed for institutional use

Read-only operation

Does not interact with plant controls or modify operations.

Human review

Supports engineering assessment rather than replacing operator judgment.

Structured findings

Highlights developing issues in a form that can be examined and challenged.

Defensible records

Preserves context for later operational and reliability review.

Accountability

Earlier insight without losing discipline

Neraium is building a new intelligence layer for industrial infrastructure based on Systemic Infrastructure Intelligence. By analyzing relationships across sensors and equipment, the platform detects changes in system behavior that signal emerging degradation before failures occur.

The goal is to help industrial operators gain earlier insight into developing problems, reduce unplanned downtime, and improve overall system reliability while keeping results reviewable in real operating contexts.
