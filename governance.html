Governance

Neraium is designed as a governance-aligned intelligence layer for critical infrastructure.

The platform provides earlier infrastructure visibility while preserving strict operational boundaries and institutional accountability.

Operational Boundary

Read Only Architecture

Neraium ingests telemetry and performs analysis without modifying infrastructure systems or operational configurations.

No System Control

The platform does not actuate pumps, valves, treatment systems, or control processes. No commands are issued to operational systems.

Operator Authority

All operational decisions remain entirely with licensed operators, infrastructure owners, and responsible engineering teams.

Evidence Discipline

Infrastructure intelligence must be defensible. Neraium structures analytical outputs so that observations can be reviewed, validated, and retained as structured operational evidence.

The Evidence Vault Protocol establishes disciplined custody of analytical outputs, ensuring that system observations are recorded with context and traceability.

System Roles

Neraium

The systemic infrastructure intelligence platform responsible for telemetry analysis and deviation detection.

Mnemosyne

Custody layer responsible for evidence retention, traceability, and structured record discipline.

Ananke

Doctrine authority defining governance principles and operational boundary conditions for infrastructure intelligence systems.

Institutional Alignment

Critical infrastructure operators must balance visibility with responsibility. Increased system insight can introduce institutional duty if intelligence is not structured carefully.

Neraium is designed to provide earlier signal detection while preserving clear boundaries between observation, interpretation, and operational action.

Neraium does not control infrastructure systems.

The platform observes, analyzes, and records system behavior. Decisions remain with human operators and infrastructure owners.

Deployment Principle

Infrastructure intelligence must integrate without destabilizing operational governance.

Neraium is deployed as a read-only analytical layer that enhances system visibility while maintaining strict separation from control systems.
