Principles

Operational Principles

The governance model defines the non‑negotiable guardrails that keep Neraium strictly read‑only and auditable, ensuring that operational control remains untouched.

Read‑Only Architecture

The platform observes infrastructure systems without exerting any control over them.

Operational Independence

Existing control systems remain unchanged and independent of the analysis layer.

Infrastructure Transparency

Operational environments become measurable and explainable through telemetry.

Accountability

Operational intelligence remains auditable and defensible, producing evidence records for review.

Boundaries

System Boundaries

The platform operates outside of operational control environments. Infrastructure authority remains fully within existing systems and human operators, ensuring that decision‑making stays in the hands of those who manage the infrastructure.
