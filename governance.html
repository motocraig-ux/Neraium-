Governance

Findings that remain
reviewable and defensible

Neraium is designed to support disciplined operational review — not uncontrolled automation, but earlier insight that engineering teams can evaluate and act on.

Principles

Intelligence that supports human judgment

The goal is not to remove operators from the loop. Neraium surfaces structural change early so that reliability and engineering teams can make better-informed decisions — with full visibility into what the system detected and why.

Governance Model

Designed for institutional use

01
Read-only Operation

Does not interact with plant controls or modify operations.

02
Human Review

Supports engineering assessment rather than replacing operator judgment.

03
Structured Findings

Highlights developing issues in a form that can be examined and challenged.

04
Defensible Records

Preserves context for later operational and reliability review.

Accountability

Outputs that can be examined and challenged

Every finding Neraium surfaces is structured to be traceable. Grounded in specific signal relationships and time windows, not opaque model outputs. Teams can interrogate what was detected, when it began, and which subsystems were involved.

This makes Neraium appropriate for environments where auditability, institutional accountability, and engineering rigor are not optional.
