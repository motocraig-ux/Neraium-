Governance

Neraium is designed as a governance-aligned infrastructure intelligence system.

The platform provides earlier visibility into infrastructure behavior while preserving strict operational boundaries and institutional accountability.

Operational Boundary

Read Only Architecture

Neraium observes and analyzes telemetry but never modifies operational infrastructure systems.

No System Control

The platform does not actuate pumps, valves, treatment processes, or control systems.

Operator Authority

All operational decisions remain with licensed operators and infrastructure owners.

Evidence Discipline

Infrastructure intelligence must remain defensible. Neraium structures analytical outputs so they can be reviewed, validated, and preserved as structured operational evidence.

The Evidence Vault Protocol ensures that admitted analytical outputs maintain traceability and contextual integrity throughout their lifecycle.

Governance Roles

Neraium

Stewardship entity responsible for governance of the infrastructure intelligence system.
