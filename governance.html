Governance

Neraium operates as a read-only infrastructure intelligence layer. The system observes telemetry and produces analytical outputs without modifying operational infrastructure or issuing control commands. Operational authority remains fully with system operators.

PRINCIPLES

Core Governance Principles

Observation Only

Neraium analyzes telemetry but does not control or modify infrastructure.

No System Actuation

The platform does not issue commands to SCADA, BAS, PLC, or operational systems.

Operator Authority

All operational decisions remain entirely with facility engineers and operators.

RESPONSIBILITY

Operational Responsibility

Neraium provides analytical intelligence derived from telemetry data. Operators review this information and determine operational responses. Existing control systems such as SCADA, BAS, and PLC infrastructure remain unchanged and outside the Neraium system boundary.

EVIDENCE

Evidence Discipline

Neraium maintains strict separation between analytical capability and operational performance claims. Demonstrations validate the analytical methods of the system. Operational performance claims require validation using real telemetry within partner environments.
