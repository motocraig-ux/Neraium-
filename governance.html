Operational Principles

Designed to support operators, not replace them

Infrastructure intelligence should improve visibility without altering the authority structure of operational systems. The Neraium platform is built around this boundary.

Read-Only Observation

The platform receives telemetry signals but does not issue commands or modify control system behavior.

Operational Independence

Existing SCADA, control logic, and automation layers remain unchanged and continue to govern system operations.

Infrastructure Transparency

Operators gain a coherent view of infrastructure behavior across multiple telemetry sources.

Accountability

All surfaced intelligence is preserved as evidence so that interpretations remain explainable and reviewable.

Governance Model

Preserving operational authority

Infrastructure intelligence must remain accountable to operators, regulators, and system stakeholders. Neraium therefore preserves a clear separation between analysis and control.

Transparent Interpretation

Every surfaced interpretation can be traced back through the signals and evaluation steps that produced it.

Evidence Preservation

Operational intelligence is recorded as reviewable evidence rather than transient system alerts.

Operator Authority

Human operators remain the final authority for all infrastructure control decisions.
