Governance

Operational intelligence with clear boundaries

Neraium is designed to inform operators without taking control. Processing can be kept private and access controlled.

Operational principles
Strong boundaries, controlled access, and operator authority are deliberate design choices.

Read-only architecture

No actuation. No control changes. No setpoint modification.

Access control

API keys, rate limiting, and audit logging on the backend.

Reviewability

Results can be preserved for review, audits, and incident response.

Boundary model
Systems remain authoritative

Inputs

Telemetry exported from existing systems.

Intelligence layer

Private processing and schema detection.

Decisions

Operators decide actions outside the platform.
