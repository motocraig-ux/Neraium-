Governance Architecture

Analytical outputs generated by Neraium are evaluated through Aletheia's Gate before institutional acknowledgement. This governance layer operates under the authority framework defined by Ananke.

Outputs that meet governance criteria are recorded through Mnemosyne, the system responsible for custody and institutional memory.

Evidence Vault Protocol operates within Mnemosyne to preserve cryptographic evidence of system observations, ensuring traceability, accountability, and defensible infrastructure intelligence.
