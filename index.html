What Neraium Does

Neraium is a read-only infrastructure intelligence layer that ingests existing telemetry from critical systems and identifies sustained structural deviation before failure occurs.

The platform focuses on deterioration signals that appear weeks or months before operational alarms, allowing operators to detect degradation earlier in the lifecycle of infrastructure assets.

How It Works

Telemetry Ingestion

Connects to existing telemetry sources such as SCADA, BAS, BMS and industrial monitoring systems without modifying equipment or control systems.

Deviation Detection

Analyzes multi-signal telemetry to identify persistent structural drift patterns that precede failure or efficiency degradation.

Operator Intelligence

Results are presented as clear analytical outputs for operators and engineers to review and act on. Neraium does not automate control decisions.
