Overview

Turning maintenance from reactive to proactive

Industrial failures rarely begin as obvious alarms. They are often preceded by subtle changes in how systems behave together across time.

Neraium analyzes those changing relationships to help teams identify developing issues earlier.

The Problem

Traditional monitoring often misses early change

Thresholds and single-sensor anomaly detection work well for obvious issues, but they can miss the early stages of developing problems in complex environments.

  • Changing relationships between power, load, and temperature
  • Cooling performance drifting relative to operating conditions
  • Unstable process dynamics across multiple subsystems
  • Mechanical systems behaving differently under varying loads
Approach

A read-only intelligence layer for industrial infrastructure

Neraium analyzes industrial systems as networks of interacting signals. When structural changes appear, the platform highlights equipment or processes that may require attention.

The goal is earlier insight, lower deployment risk, and more disciplined review.
