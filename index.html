NERAIUM

Systemic Infrastructure Intelligence

Neraium is a read-only infrastructure intelligence layer. It ingests telemetry, analyzes sustained deviation patterns, and produces reviewable records without issuing control commands.

Platform

Neraium operates as a read-only analytical layer for physical infrastructure. It evaluates telemetry and produces contextual outputs for operator review.

Telemetry Ingestion

Receives existing system telemetry without sending control signals.

Deviation Analysis

Detects sustained multi-signal drift relative to baseline behavior.

Review Records

Preserves context-rich outputs for operator interpretation.

Evidence Basis
