What Neraium Does

Neraium ingests telemetry from pumps, treatment systems, cooling loops, and water infrastructure.

The platform identifies sustained structural deviation across sensor streams long before system failure occurs.

Neraium never controls equipment. The system is strictly read-only and designed for infrastructure intelligence.
