Overview

Infrastructure Intelligence

Neraium ingests telemetry from critical infrastructure and detects sustained structural deviation before failure occurs. The platform operates strictly in read-only mode and never issues control commands or modifies infrastructure.

Detection

Identify sustained structural deviation before operational alarms occur.

Governance

Outputs pass through deterministic governance before institutional acknowledgement.

Authority

Operators retain full authority over infrastructure systems.

Architecture

System Architecture

Neraium sits above operational infrastructure as a read-only intelligence layer. Analytical output passes through governance before institutional acknowledgement.

Telemetry Ingestion

BAS, SCADA, and historian signals are ingested without modifying infrastructure behavior.

Deviation Detection

Signals are analyzed for sustained structural deviation across operational systems.

Governance Gate

Analytical outputs pass through Aletheia's Gate under Ananke governance before institutional acknowledgement.

Boundary

System Boundary

Neraium operates strictly as an infrastructure intelligence layer. The system analyzes telemetry and produces governed analytical output, but it does not control infrastructure systems.

Read Only

Neraium ingests operational telemetry but does not write commands or modify system state.

No Control Layer

The platform does not replace or override existing control systems such as BAS or SCADA.

Operator Authority

Infrastructure operators retain full authority over system decisions and actions.

Outcomes

Operational Outcomes

Neraium enables infrastructure operators to detect structural deviation earlier, respond to deterioration sooner, and maintain institutional accountability for operational decisions.

Earlier Detection

Structural deterioration signals appear before traditional alarms or system failures.

Reduced Reactive Maintenance

Operators gain visibility into emerging system conditions before they escalate into urgent repairs.

Defensible Intelligence

Governance architecture ensures analytical outputs are traceable, auditable, and institutionally accountable.
