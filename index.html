Infrastructure Intelligence

Neraium analyzes multivariate telemetry across operational environments to detect structural deviation before failure events occur.

The Problem

Industrial systems, water networks, cooling infrastructure, manufacturing systems, and operational facilities rely on telemetry and monitoring tools to detect problems. However most monitoring systems are reactive. They rely on thresholds and alarms triggered only after a system has already deviated significantly from expected behavior.

By the time a traditional alarm triggers, deterioration has already progressed, often leading to downtime, maintenance emergencies, or system failure.

A Different Approach

Neraium analyzes telemetry environments using multivariate analysis rather than simple thresholds. Instead of evaluating individual metrics, the system evaluates relationships between variables across entire infrastructure environments.

This approach allows the platform to detect structural system drift that would otherwise remain invisible to conventional monitoring tools.

Multivariate Telemetry Analysis

System behavior is evaluated across multiple operational variables simultaneously, allowing subtle deviations to be identified early.

Structural Drift Detection

The platform identifies gradual infrastructure deterioration before threshold alarms would normally trigger.

Operational Intelligence

Operators gain insight into system health across entire infrastructure networks rather than individual equipment.

Read-Only Operation

Neraium observes infrastructure environments without controlling equipment or modifying system behavior.
