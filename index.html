Infrastructure Intelligence

Neraium provides systemic intelligence for complex infrastructure environments. We analyze multivariate telemetry from operational systems to detect structural deviation and deterioration before failure occurs.

What Neraium Does

Most infrastructure monitoring systems focus on alarms and thresholds. Neraium analyzes entire telemetry environments simultaneously, identifying structural drift and system behavior changes long before operational failure occurs.

Multivariate Analysis

Instead of single metrics or thresholds, Neraium evaluates entire telemetry environments across pumps, exchangers, cooling loops and other infrastructure.

Early Structural Detection

The platform identifies sustained deviation patterns weeks or months before traditional monitoring tools detect failures.

Operational Intelligence

Operators gain visibility into system behavior, degradation patterns and efficiency losses across complex infrastructure networks.

Read Only Architecture

Neraium provides intelligence without controlling equipment. It observes and analyzes systems while leaving operational control unchanged.

Where It Is Used

Neraium is designed for environments where infrastructure failure carries significant operational or financial impact.

Industrial Manufacturing

Process cooling loops, pumping systems and production infrastructure.

Data Centers

Cooling infrastructure, chilled water systems and heat rejection systems.

Hospitality

Large resort and hospitality water infrastructure environments.

Municipal Infrastructure

Water treatment systems, pumping stations and infrastructure networks.
