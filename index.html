What Neraium Does

Neraium is a read only infrastructure intelligence layer that ingests existing telemetry from critical systems and identifies sustained structural deviation before failure occurs.

The platform focuses on deterioration signals that appear weeks or months before operational alarms, allowing operators to detect degradation earlier in the lifecycle of infrastructure assets.

How It Works

Telemetry Ingestion

Connects to existing telemetry environments such as BAS, SCADA, and industrial monitoring systems without modifying infrastructure.

Deviation Detection

Analyzes telemetry patterns to identify sustained structural drift that precedes system failure or degradation.

Operator Intelligence

Outputs are presented as clear analytical signals for engineers and operators to evaluate and act upon.

Platform Architecture

Telemetry Ingestion

Secure ingestion from BAS, SCADA and industrial telemetry systems.

Signal Analysis

Multi signal telemetry analysis identifies structural deterioration patterns.

Evidence Generation

Outputs are structured as reviewable intelligence for operator review.

Operator Authority

Neraium does not automate decisions or modify infrastructure systems.
