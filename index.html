The Problem

Why reactive systems fail

Water and infrastructure failures are rarely sudden. They emerge from long periods of subtle drift across pressure, flow, treatment, and control conditions. Threshold-based monitoring often sees the symptom late instead of understanding the system early.

Reactive operations

Isolated alarms trigger after degradation is already underway. Operators inherit fragmented signals, incomplete context, and limited system-level visibility.

Neraium

Structural deviation is identified early, system behavior becomes interpretable, and surfaced intelligence remains defensible.

Capabilities

Neraium is not just a monitoring layer. It is an intelligence layer that helps operators move from disconnected telemetry to coherent operational understanding.
