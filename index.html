Architecture

System Architecture

Neraium sits above operational infrastructure as a read-only intelligence layer. Analytical output passes through layered governance before any institutional acknowledgement is issued.

01

Telemetry Ingestion

BAS, SCADA, and historian signals are ingested passively — infrastructure behavior is never modified.

02

Deviation Detection

Signals are continuously analyzed for sustained structural deviation across operational systems.

03

Governance Gate

Analytical outputs pass through Aletheia's Gate under Ananke governance before institutional acknowledgement.
