Methodology

How results are produced

This page explains evaluation assumptions and how pilots are validated in live environments.

Data Basis

Evaluation datasets

  • Public simulation datasets (for example, CMAPSS variants) are used for controlled evaluation.
  • Evaluation focuses on lead-time behavior and structural drift signals before failure labels.
  • Results are reported as evaluation indicators and should be validated in your environment.

Assumptions

What the numbers mean

  • Lead-time values are measured in dataset-native units (for example, cycles).
  • Detection metrics depend on baseline windows, persistence gates, and drift thresholds.
  • Dataset operating conditions may not match your exact environment or failure profile.

Pilot Validation

How production value is measured

  • Scope to one target system with documented operator risk priorities.
  • Track early-warning findings against known incidents/events in your environment.
  • Report measured warning windows, confidence indicators, and actionability outcomes.
