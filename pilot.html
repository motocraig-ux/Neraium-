Timeline

Four phases across 60 days

Week 1-2

Integration and baseline setup

Connect read-only telemetry, align context windows, and establish structural baseline.

Week 3-4

Drift detection and signal review

Run detection pipeline and review high-signal relationship shifts with your operators.

Week 5-6

Lead-time validation

Compare findings against known incidents or controlled events to measure warning window.

Week 7-8

Decision package

Deliver rollout recommendation, operating playbook, and next-facility deployment plan.
