The Neraium Platform

The Neraium platform implements Systemic Infrastructure Intelligence by analyzing relationships between telemetry signals across infrastructure environments.

Platform Overview

Modern infrastructure systems generate continuous streams of telemetry data. Most monitoring tools analyze these signals individually rather than as interconnected operational systems.

The Neraium platform evaluates telemetry relationships across entire infrastructure environments to identify emerging system drift.

Platform Components

Telemetry Ingestion

Operational signals are collected from infrastructure systems.

System Modeling

Infrastructure environments are modeled as operational networks.

Multivariate Analysis

Relationships between variables reveal structural system behavior.

Deviation Detection

Structural drift patterns are detected before failure occurs.
