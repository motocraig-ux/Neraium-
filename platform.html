Validated approach

Tested on real engines and industrial systems

Neraium's structural instability detection is validated against known failure histories. CMAPSS: 96.5% coverage, 181-cycle median lead time. FD004: 94.4% coverage, 211-cycle lead time. Pilots measure performance on your own systems against your documented failures.

Where it fits

Deploy beside your monitoring stack

Neraium operates in read-only mode beside your existing instrumentation, historians, and monitoring tools. Start small in one environment, validate lead time, then expand once your team trusts the findings.

Why structure

See degradation before thresholds move

Threshold alerts are essential, but they often trigger after damage accumulates. Structural monitoring surfaces the relationship shifts that appear earlier, while systems still look nominal in absolute terms.
