Platform

A read-only intelligence layer for industrial infrastructure

Neraium analyzes existing plant telemetry to identify structural changes in system behavior before failures occur, without interacting with control systems or modifying plant operations.

The system is designed to sit alongside existing monitoring infrastructure, allowing teams to evaluate systemic behavior with minimal deployment risk.

Platform Structure

Three parts of the platform

Telemetry Intake

Ingests existing sensor and process data from plant environments.

System Analysis

Evaluates changing relationships across signals over time.

Actionable Output

Highlights equipment or processes that may require earlier attention.

Where This Applies

Designed for complex industrial environments

  • Steel production and melt shops
  • Heavy manufacturing facilities
  • Energy and power infrastructure
  • Large mechanical and thermal systems
