The Neraium Platform

The Neraium platform transforms operational telemetry into systemic infrastructure intelligence. Instead of isolated monitoring tools, the platform analyzes entire telemetry environments to detect structural system behavior changes.

Platform Architecture

Neraium integrates telemetry ingestion, multivariate analysis, and governed intelligence outputs into a unified platform designed for infrastructure environments.

Telemetry Ingestion

Operational telemetry from pumps, exchangers, cooling loops and other infrastructure is ingested directly into the analysis environment.

Multivariate Detection

System drift is identified by analyzing patterns across multiple operational variables instead of single threshold alarms.

Infrastructure Mapping

Systems are mapped as operational networks rather than isolated equipment.

Intelligence Output

Operators receive early insight into system deterioration and efficiency loss.

Designed for Complex Systems

Infrastructure environments such as industrial plants, cooling systems, and water networks operate as interconnected systems. Neraium analyzes these environments as complete operational structures rather than individual assets.
