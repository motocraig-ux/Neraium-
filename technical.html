Technical

Identifying structural change before failures become visible

Neraium analyzes how signals across an industrial system interact over time. When those relationships begin to shift, it can indicate that degradation or instability is emerging even when individual sensors still appear normal.

Technical Approach

What the system evaluates continuously

Signal relationships

Relationships between sensors across equipment and processes.

Behavior over time

Stability of system behavior as operating conditions change.

Emerging patterns

Patterns that may indicate degradation or growing instability.

Operating dynamics

Shifts in multi-signal behavior that do not appear in isolated thresholds.

Example

An example of structural drift appearing before failure

This example shows how systemic drift can emerge well before the actual failure point. The goal is earlier visibility into developing problems, not just confirmation after failure is already obvious.

Why It Matters

A better view of developing industrial problems

In complex industrial environments, failures frequently begin with subtle shifts in system behavior. By identifying these changes earlier, Neraium helps operations and reliability teams detect developing problems before equipment failures occur.
