Identifying structural change

Neraium analyzes signal relationships, not isolated thresholds, detecting drift in how systems behave together.

How It Works

Signal relationships reveal what thresholds miss

Neraium analyzes how signals across an industrial system interact over time. When those relationships begin to shift, it can indicate that degradation or instability is emerging, even when individual sensors still appear normal.
