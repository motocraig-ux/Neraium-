Neraium Technical Overview

Neraium is a read-only infrastructure intelligence layer that ingests telemetry from critical physical systems and detects sustained structural deviation before failure occurs.

System Boundary

Neraium does not control equipment and does not modify operational systems. Operators retain full authority over infrastructure environments.

Deviation Detection

The platform analyzes multi-signal telemetry and identifies persistent drift patterns that precede failure or efficiency degradation.
