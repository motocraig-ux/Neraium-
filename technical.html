Built to interpret system behavior, not just trigger threshold alarms

Neraium evaluates whether industrial systems remain physically coherent across time, using relationships between signals rather than treating telemetry as disconnected events.

[Technical Architecture]

Interpretation Structure

Multivariate Geometry

Detects structural drift from relationships between signals, not isolated variable breaches.

Temporal Legitimacy

Requires persistence over time before a detected deformation can progress into escalation.

Physical Coherence

Constrains interpretation to engineering-valid meaning and admissible abstraction boundaries.

Read-Only Deployment

Operates alongside existing control environments without issuing directives or modifying control logic.

Why This Matters

  • Earlier awareness: identify systemic drift before conventional alarms become obvious.
  • Explainable outputs: each interpretation is bounded by explicit admissibility logic.
  • Operational fit: works alongside SCADA, historians, and established monitoring environments.
  • Defensible review: preserves records for engineering analysis, compliance review, and incident investigation.
