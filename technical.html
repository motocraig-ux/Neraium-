Technical Specification

System Boundary and Analytical Framework

Neraium functions as a read only infrastructure intelligence layer. It ingests telemetry, performs multivariate deviation modeling, and generates structured analytical outputs. The platform does not execute control actions or modify operational systems.

01 System Boundary
Operational Scope
The platform evaluates telemetry without issuing commands. Authority remains fully with existing operational control systems.

Read Only Telemetry

Signals are received passively and processed analytically.

No Actuation

No downstream control authority or system modification capability.

Structured Evidence

Analytical state preserved for governance and review processes.
