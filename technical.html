Technical

Composable architecture with observable internals.

The platform is organized around adapters, a normalization plane, a topology model, an analysis plane, and a decision surface. Each component can operate independently or as part of the full Neraium system.

Adapters

Pluggable collectors support logs, metrics, events, files, and domain-specific telemetry feeds.

Normalization plane

Signals are transformed into a shared event schema for routing, scoring, and lineage capture.

Analysis plane

Supports thresholding, drift detection, covariance methods, and operator-configured decision rules.

Decision surface

Outputs are ranked, explained, and passed through governance checkpoints before actioning.
