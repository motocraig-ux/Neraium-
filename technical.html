Technical Overview

Detection Architecture Without Control Authority

Neraium is architected as a read-only intelligence layer. It ingests telemetry, computes sustained multivariate deviation, and produces reviewable records. It does not actuate, modify, or control operational systems.

Architecture
Three Layer Model
Separation of ingestion, computation, and review ensures operator authority remains intact while analytical signal quality increases.
Layer 01

Ingest

Telemetry normalization, timestamp alignment, baseline window definition.

Layer 02

Analyze

Multivariate distance computation, smoothing, deviation gating, lead time quantification.

Layer 03

Review

Structured analytical records for operator response and investor diligence.

Detection Logic
Deviation Based Classification
The system measures sustained structural divergence from a defined baseline. Admission requires persistence, not transient noise.
Baseline

Initial Stability Window

First operational period defines mean and covariance parameters.

Distance

Multivariate Metric

Mahalanobis distance measures structural deviation across signals.

Admission

Persistence Requirement

Deviation must persist across windowed smoothing before classification.

Operators
Operator Authority Preserved
Neraium never executes control commands. It surfaces earlier signal so licensed professionals can make informed decisions.
Investors
Diligence Signal
Structured analytical records expose deterioration trajectory before consequence. This informs capital planning and asset underwriting.
