Built for multivariate telemetry, not isolated threshold alarms

Neraium evaluates whether the overall behaviour of a system remains physically coherent rather than treating sensor streams as disconnected signals.

Interpretation Structure

Multivariate Geometry

Detects structural drift from relationships between sensor signals rather than single variable thresholds.

Temporal Legitimacy

Requires persistence over time before deformation can progress into escalation.

Physical Coherence

Constrains interpretation to valid engineering meaning and admissible abstraction boundaries.

Read Only Deployment

Operates at the edge without modifying control systems or issuing directives.

Why This Matters

  • Earlier awareness: detect drift before failure states become obvious in conventional alarms.
  • Explainable outputs: every interpretation is bounded by explicit admissibility logic.
  • Operational fit: works alongside SCADA, historian, and existing monitoring environments.
  • Defensible review: preserves records for engineering analysis, compliance review, and post incident investigation.
