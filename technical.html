Neraium Technical Overview

Neraium is a read only infrastructure intelligence platform that analyzes telemetry signals to identify sustained structural deviation before system failure occurs.

System Boundary

Neraium does not control equipment, automate infrastructure systems, or modify operational environments. The platform operates strictly as an observational intelligence layer.

Deviation Detection

Multi signal telemetry streams are analyzed to identify persistent patterns of deterioration and system drift that emerge prior to operational alarms.
