Interpretation Model

Neraium operates as a read only intelligence layer across live telemetry environments. Sustained structural deviation is mapped for persistence and structured for operator review.

Model Structure

Live Telemetry
Signal Normalization
Cross Signal Correlation
Deviation Persistence Mapping
Structured Signal Interpretation
Operator Review

Signal Integrity

Outputs include signal lineage, temporal persistence, and contextual metadata. Interpretation artifacts are surfaced without asserting operational outcomes or triggering automated control.

Boundary

Neraium does not actuate, modify, override, or control infrastructure systems. Decision authority remains exclusively with operators.
