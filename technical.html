Signal Integrity

Outputs preserve signal lineage, temporal persistence, and contextual metadata. Interpretation artifacts are surfaced without asserting operational outcomes. Every output remains traceable to source signals and temporal context.

04

Institutional Framework

Neraium structures outputs for institutional review, liability, and oversight. Traceable artifacts preserve lineage, persistence, and context so governance remains explicit at the point of decision. All intelligence remains read-only.

05

Operational Boundary

Neraium does not actuate, modify, override, or control infrastructure systems. Decision authority remains exclusively with water operators. The system observes, analyzes, and communicates. Operators decide.
